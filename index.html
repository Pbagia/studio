About the Artist

I make slow objects in a fast world — surfaces that ask to be read with the eyes the way the hand would read them.

Prina Bagia is a material artist working between weaving, paper casting, and drawing. Trained first as a textile designer, her practice has moved steadily toward the sculptural — pieces that sit somewhere between the loom and the wall.

Her work is held in private collections across North America and has been shown in group exhibitions focused on craft, material, and the handmade mark. She lives and works from her home studio on the West Coast.

— Prina
